6. Data Security and Protection

6.1 Security Measures

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered or disclosed:

6.2 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based access, principle of least privilege
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, DDoS protection
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring, automated threat detection, regular security audits

6.3 Physical and Operational Security

  • Secure data centers with biometric access controls
  • Regular security training for all employees
  • Incident response plans and breach notification procedures
  • Regular backups with offsite storage and encryption

6.4 Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for:

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Transaction Data: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized after 26 months

7.1 Safeguards for International Transfers

When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Binding corporate rules for intra-group transfers
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

7.2 Specific Transfer Mechanisms

We use the following mechanisms for international data transfers:

  • EU-US Privacy Shield: For transfers to certified US companies
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: For transfers to non-adequate countries
  • Binding Corporate Rules: For intra-group international transfers

8. Your Legal Rights and Choices

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data:

8.1 Access Rights

  • Right of Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Confirmation: Confirm if we are processing your data
  • Right to Information: Receive information about our processing activities

8.2 Correction and Modification Rights

  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Completion: Complete incomplete personal data
  • Right to Update: Update your information through your account

8.3 Erasure and Restriction Rights

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

8.4 Data Portability and Withdraw Rights

  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing
  • Right to Object to Marketing: Object to direct marketing communications

12. Data Breach Notification

We are committed to notifying you and relevant authorities in the event of a data breach that affects your personal information.

12.1 Breach Response Plan

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Assess the nature and scope of the breach within 24 hours
  • Contain the breach and prevent further unauthorized access
  • Evaluate the risks to affected individuals
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours if required by law
  • Notify relevant supervisory authorities as required
  • Provide guidance on protective steps users can take

12.2 Notification Timeline

We will notify you:

  • High Risk Breaches: Within 72 hours of discovery
  • Medium Risk Breaches: Within 30 days
  • Low Risk Breaches: In our next regular security update
